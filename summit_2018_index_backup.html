DEVCON CDO DEVELOPER SURVEY 2018

We’re curious on how the Developer Community is growing in Northern Mindanao. This survey is open to all kinds of developers -- students and professionals alike in the IT industry.

This will last until November 5 and the results of the study will be presented at the DevCon Summit 2018: CDO Edition on November 10.

Why take the survey?

  • Developer Demographics
    What are the different roles of the developers in Northern Mindanao? What is their background, their education, years of experience, age, and so on?
  • Technical Knowledge
    What technologies are being used by developers in Northern Mindanao? What version control, methodologies, languages, platforms, and databases are being used and how competent are they with them?
  • Overview of Work
    When assessing work opportunities, what factors are developers looking for? What is the average compensation? How is this influenced by years of experience, education, technical knowledge, and competency in skillset?

In this study, we’re not only trying to understand the current state of the Developer Community, but we’re also aiming to inform companies and organizations of the opportunities and talents available in Northern Mindanao.
